Travel Book Website: FDM Travel
Technologies Employed
  • Angular Framework and Package Management
  • Java, Microservices
  • My SQL
This website is utilised by a Danish company to facilitate the booking of travel itineraries, encompassing both air travel, ground transportation, and accommodations. Our primary responsibility was to develop common components for the website, and we successfully completed the backend development phase.
